# Ubiquitous Language — Company Domain

> Vietnamese SME accounting system. Company module terminology.
> Regulatory base: Luật Doanh nghiệp 2024 (LDN 2024), NĐ 168/2025/NĐ-CP, TT 99/2025/TT-BTC.

---

## Company Core

| Term (VN) | Term (EN) | Definition | Aliases to avoid |
|-----------|-----------|------------|------------------|
| Doanh nghiệp | Enterprise | Organization with own name, assets, registered address, legally established for business purposes (LDN Điều 4) | Company, Business |
| Mã số doanh nghiệp | Enterprise Code | 10-digit unique identifier issued by business registration authority (NĐ 168/2025 Điều 6) | Business ID, Company code |
| Mã số thuế | Tax Code | 10 or 13-digit tax identification number; first 2 digits = province code | MST, Tax ID |
| Vốn điều lệ | Chartered Capital | Total value of assets contributed by members/shareholders as recorded in company charter | Registered capital |
| Vốn pháp định | Legal Capital | Minimum capital required by law for specific industries (e.g. banking, real estate) | Minimum capital |
| Loại hình doanh nghiệp | Enterprise Type | Legal form: DNTN, LLC-1member, LLC-2members, JSC, Partnership, Sole proprietorship | Company type |
| Người đại diện theo pháp luật | Legal Representative | Individual representing the enterprise in legal matters (LDN Điều 12-16) | Legal rep, Statutory rep |
| Ngành nghề kinh doanh | Business Line | Registered economic activity classified by VSIC code | Industry, Sector |
| Trụ sở chính | Head Office | Registered address of the enterprise as per business registration certificate | HQ, Main office |
| Chi nhánh | Branch | Dependent unit of the enterprise with separate tax code; can conduct business operations | Branch office |

| Địa điểm kinh doanh | Business Location | Sub-unit under head office or branch where actual business is conducted | Operational site |
| Cổng thông tin quốc gia về đăng ký doanh nghiệp | National Business Registration Portal | Centralized online system for enterprise registration and filing (DKKD) | National portal, Business registry |
| Hệ thống đăng ký thuế | Tax Registration System | National tax authority system for tax code issuance and management | Tax portal |

## Relationships

- A **Doanh nghiệp** has exactly 1 **Mã số doanh nghiệp** (unique, immutable)
- A **Doanh nghiệp** has exactly 1 **Mã số thuế** (may differ from Enterprise Code)
- A **Doanh nghiệp** has 1 or many **Người đại diện theo pháp luật** (at least 1 required)
- A **Doanh nghiệp** has 1 or many **Ngành nghề kinh doanh** with VSIC codes
- A **Doanh nghiệp** has 0 or many **Chi nhánh** / **Văn phòng đại diện** / **Địa điểm kinh doanh**
- A **Chi nhánh** has exactly 1 **Mã số thuế riêng** (separate branch tax code)
- A **Doanh nghiệp** has 0 or many **Tài khoản ngân hàng** (1 required for tax payments)
- A **Doanh nghiệp** has exactly 1 **Chế độ kế toán** and 1 **Năm tài chính**
- A **Doanh nghiệp** has exactly 1 **Trạng thái** at any time (state machine)
- A **Doanh nghiệp** has exactly 1 **Loại hình doanh nghiệp** (fixed at incorporation)
- A **Doanh nghiệp** employs 0 or many **Người dùng** (accounting staff, via User-Company assignment)
- A **Doanh nghiệp** has exactly 1 **Kế toán trưởng** (current assigned person)
- A **Doanh nghiệp** has 0 or many **Cổ đông** / **Thành viên góp vốn**
- A **Công ty cổ phần** has 1 **Hội đồng quản trị** and 1 **Ban kiểm soát** (if > 11 shareholders)

## Company State Machine

```
                    ┌────────────┐
                    │  Đăng ký   │
                    │(Registered)│
                    └─────┬──────┘
                          │
                          ▼
                    ┌────────────┐
           ┌───────▶│  Đang hoạt │◀──────┐
           │        │   động     │       │
           │        │  (Active)  │       │
           │        └─────┬──────┘       │
           │              │              │
           │    ┌─────────┴────────┐     │
           │    ▼                  ▼     │
           │ ┌────────┐     ┌──────────┐ │
           │ │Tạm      │     │  Thu hồi  │ │
           │ │ngừng    │     │  giấy     │ │
           │ │(Suspended)   │  phép     │ │
           │ │         │     │(Revoked) │ │
           │ └────┬────┘     └────┬─────┘ │
           │      │               │       │
           └──────┘               ▼       │
                            ┌────────────┐ │
                            │  Giải thể  │ │
                            │(Dissolved) │ │
                            └────────────┘ │
                            ┌────────────┐ │
                            │  Phá sản   │─┘
                            │ (Bankrupt) │
                            └────────────┘
```

Transitions:
- **Registered** → **Active**: Business registration approved
- **Active** ↔ **Suspended**: Company may suspend and resume operations
- **Active** → **Dissolved**: Voluntary or compulsory dissolution
- **Active** → **Bankrupt**: Court bankruptcy declaration
- **Active** → **Revoked**: License revoked by authority
- **Suspended** → **Dissolved**: Company dissolves while suspended
- **Suspended** → **Bankrupt**: Court declares bankruptcy during suspension
- **Revoked** → **Dissolved**: Company must dissolve within 6 months of revocation

## Example Dialogue

> **BA:** "When a **Doanh nghiệp** changes its **Người đại diện theo pháp luật**, do we need to update the **Cổng thông tin quốc gia về đăng ký doanh nghiệp**?"
> **Domain Expert:** "Yes, per NĐ 168/2025 Điều 35, the change must be published within 10 days. Also the **Tài khoản ngân hàng** for tax payments linked to the old rep must be reverified."
> **BA:** "What about the company's **Chế độ kế toán** — can the **Kế toán trưởng** change from TT99 to TT133 mid-year?"
> **Domain Expert:** "No. The **Chế độ kế toán** is locked at the start of the **Năm tài chính**. Any change takes effect from the next fiscal year. This is per TT 99/2025/TT-BTC."

> **BA:** "A **Công ty TNHH hai thành viên** wants to convert to **Công ty cổ phần**. How does this affect the accounting setup?"
> **Domain Expert:** "The **Loại hình doanh nghiệp** changes, which means a new **Mã số doanh nghiệp** is issued. However, the **Mã số thuế** remains the same. The **Vốn điều lệ** becomes **Vốn cổ phần**. The **Chế độ kế toán** does not reset—it continues."
> **BA:** "So the **Năm tài chính** and **Kỳ kế toán** carry over?"
> **Domain Expert:** "Correct. Only the legal form changes. The accounting period continuity is preserved."

> **BA:** "When a **Chi nhánh** files its **Báo cáo thuế GTGT**, does it use its own **Mã số thuế** or the parent company's?"
> **Domain Expert:** "The **Chi nhánh** has its own **Mã số thuế riêng** (branch tax code) and files its own **Báo cáo thuế GTGT** if it has independent accounting. If dependent, the parent consolidates. Per TT 99 Điều 34."

## Flagged Ambiguities

- **"Company"** is used ambiguously in the codebase: sometimes refers to the legal entity (doanh nghiệp), sometimes to a tenant instance. Recommend: use **Doanh nghiệp** / **Enterprise** for legal entity, and **Tenant** for multi-tenancy.
- **"Settings"** in current code includes both accounting configuration and operational preferences. Recommend splitting: **CompanySettings** (accounting/fiscal config) and **CompanyPreferences** (operational).
- **TaxCode** in `Company.cs` conflates two concepts: **Mã số doanh nghiệp** (enterprise code from registration) and **Mã số thuế** (tax identification number). Per NĐ 168/2025, these are now separate. Recommend adding **EnterpriseCode** field.
- **Branch** in codebase sometimes refers to **Chi nhánh** (registered branch with separate tax code) and sometimes to **Địa điểm kinh doanh** (business location without separate tax code). These have distinct legal implications. Recommend: rename **Branch** → **LegalBranch** (chi nhánh), add **BusinessLocation** (địa điểm kinh doanh).
- **Industry** in the code uses internal categories, not the official **VSIC** (Hệ thống ngành kinh tế Việt Nam) codes. Recommend migration to VSIC for regulatory reporting compliance.
- **Status** field uses 3 states (Active/Inactive/Deleted) but the legal lifecycle requires 6+ states. Recommend implementing the full state machine above.
